About

Product thinking

I'm a Product Manager who builds things and measures what matters. I conceived and launched TryCareerMatch end-to-end — discovery, data model, UX, and deployment — a career platform mapping 88 skills against 60+ roles across 9 sectors.

My background as a Financial Analyst at ABB Imobiliária means I don't just read dashboards — I build them. Power BI, Python, SQL, KPI frameworks across a multi-company group. I know how executive decisions are made, and I can set up the metrics infrastructure most teams rely on Data to provide.

Seven years of design gives me a UX instinct and the ability to prototype quickly. I'm looking for a Product Manager role where analytical rigour and design thinking aren't separate departments — they're the same mindset.

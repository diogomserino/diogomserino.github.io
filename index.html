About me

A non-linear path
with a clear thread

My journey might look non-linear at first glance — but there's a consistent thread: I've always worked at the intersection of creativity, technology, and business.

I started in design and 3D visualisation, moved into digital marketing, and found my professional home in financial and data analysis — where these skills combine most powerfully. At ABB Imobiliária I managed KPIs across a multi-company group, built executive Power BI dashboards, and automated reporting processes that had been done manually for years.

Today I build end-to-end analytics projects and consult for SMEs. I use AI professionally to multiply my output. I think in systems, communicate in stories, and care deeply about design.

Overview

A BA's working day,
fully simulated

Fund governance is one of the most process-intensive roles in asset management — yet practical training resources are scarce. This platform bridges the gap between theory and the daily reality of a Business Analyst overseeing investment funds.

It simulates eight real workflows: from NAV reconciliation and UCITS compliance monitoring to KYC decisions, fee validation, and board pack preparation. Every simulation includes real data formats, editable tables, decision engines, and immediate feedback.

The platform is fully bilingual (EN/PT), works offline with zero dependencies, and includes a 38-chapter reference guide covering the complete fund governance lifecycle.

Task Simulations

Eight real workflows

Each simulation mirrors an actual analyst task — with real data, real decisions, and real consequences.

Simulation 01
NAV Reconciliation
Reconcile Net Asset Values across six funds using editable tables. Identify discrepancies, classify their source (pricing, FX, accrual), and produce a reconciliation report.
Simulation 02
UCITS Compliance Monitoring
Investigate breach notifications against UCITS investment restrictions. Classify each breach by severity and produce a remediation action log within the regulatory deadline.
Simulation 03
KYC / AML Decision Engine
Process investor onboarding using a KYC decision tree. Assess risk profiles, apply AML screening criteria, and approve, flag, or escalate each application.
Simulation 04
Fee Validation
Validate management and performance fee calculations including high-water mark mechanics. Spot calculation errors before they reach investors.
Simulation 05
Board Pack Preparation
Assemble a fund board pack from raw data. Select the right metrics, compose the fund performance narrative, and structure the pack for a board meeting.
Simulations 06 – 08
Additional Workflows
Plus three further simulations covering investor reporting, liquidity risk monitoring, and regulatory filing preparation. All with quizzes, scoring, and progress persistence.
Reference Guide

38 chapters, 10 parts

The complete fund governance knowledge base — from regulatory foundations to career development paths.

Parts 1 – 3
Foundations & Regulation
Fund industry foundations, UCITS and AIF structures, EU/UK regulatory frameworks (AIFMD, MiFID II, EMIR). Licensing, passporting, and ManCo obligations.
Parts 4 – 6
Operations & Risk
ManCo day-to-day operations, NAV pricing, distribution, transfer agency, and depositary relationships. Risk management frameworks, liquidity risk, and operational controls.
Parts 7 – 8
The BA Role & Analytics
The full Business Analyst scope in fund governance: workflow ownership, escalation paths, stakeholder management, and financial analysis applied to fund data.
Parts 9 – 10
Technology & Career
Data management and automation in fund operations. Career paths, certifications (CFA, CAIA, IMC), and real-world case studies with worked solutions.

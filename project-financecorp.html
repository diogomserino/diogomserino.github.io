Overview

Financial group analytics,
end to end

AurumCap Financial Group is a fictional multi-brand financial services company operating three business units. This project builds the entire analytics stack from scratch — from synthetic data generation to a boardroom-ready executive presentation.

The data models a realistic three-year performance arc (2022–2024): 2,800 transactions, up to 608 unique clients, €4.8M in cumulative revenue, and a consistent 45.1% profit margin — with strong Q4 seasonality and accelerated growth in the final year.

Every layer is owned: the Python data generator, the SQL star schema, the 20 analytical queries, the Power BI dashboard, the Excel model, and the PowerPoint executive deck.

Methodology

From raw data to executive deck

Four stages, each producing a distinct deliverable.

01
Data Generation
Python scripts generate realistic synthetic data: 2,800 transactions, 42–608 clients per year, products, regions, and monthly KPI metrics. Controlled randomisation ensures realistic seasonality and growth curves.
02
SQL Star Schema
A relational star schema normalises clients, products, and transactions. 20 analytical SQL queries cover revenue analysis, margin decomposition, YoY comparisons, product mix, and client segmentation.
03
EDA & Visualisation
Python EDA with pandas, matplotlib, and seaborn produces 11 standalone charts — heatmaps, YoY growth bars, KPI card grids, and margin waterfall breakdowns — exported as publication-quality PNGs.
04
BI & Reporting
Five-page Power BI dashboard with DAX measures. Excel financial model with pivot tables and custom charts. PowerPoint executive presentation with annotated insights ready for boardroom delivery.
